Privacy Policy

Last updated: Thursday, July 25, 2024

Creative Maps is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose personal data.

What Information Do We Collect?

We collect the following types of information:

  • Name and contact information (email address, phone number, etc.)
  • Design preferences and requirements
  • Communication history (emails, phone calls, etc.)

How Do We Use Your Information?

We use your information to:

  • Provide interior design services
  • Communicate with you about your project
  • Improve our services and website

Who Do We Share Your Information With?

We do not share your information with third parties except:

  • With your consent
  • To comply with laws and regulations
  • To protect our rights and interests

How Do We Protect Your Information?

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, and disclosure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the updated policy on our website.
